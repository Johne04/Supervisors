Security

Employees personal data is very sensitive and we urge all Supervisors to treat the data on TMS as highly confidential and to ensure that when you are not using TMS, that you log out of it before leaving your computer. For those that have their computers on production floor/ open office, please be aware of anyone near you that can view information on TMS as we need to ensure we maintain confidentiality at all times. We also ask that you change your password regularly and suggest that you change it each time you are prompted to change your email password.
